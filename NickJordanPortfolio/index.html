The Life of a Content Creator

I began my content creation journey all the way back in my 7th grade year in middle school. My school provided a game production/programming class that I decided to take and barely understand at the time. Fast forward to my sophomore year of highschool found myself at a new school where I joined the Digital Media program and the TV Production program. I was an active part of these programs through the rest of my highschool career and even got the title of school new show producer. After graduating highschool I decided to study Digital Media at Seminole State College before being able to transfer to UCF with my AA degree.

Introduction to Web Design

The last class I had taken at Seminole State College was a class soley based on the intro to HTML and CSS and strangely this was my favorite class throughout all of my time at SSC. I created multiple websites in this class (not very good when I look back on them now) that I had a ton of fun creating. Because of this class my original degree planned changed, as I was originally going to go into the game design track of Digital Media but I found more web design to be more interesting.

My time at UCF

My past/first semester at UCF has been the most work I've ever endured in a semester of school. I took 4 project based classes that included Intro to C, Social Platforms, Intro to Game Art, and Front End Web Design. As a content creator this semester has taught me so many new things, even while being online the entire time. I've learned many art techniques, lots of programming and most importantly I've gained the skill to manage my own time.

What's Next?

I look forward to the future and what's to come next as my journey as a content creator/web designer continues. In the future I'm planning on learning other coding and programming languages like JavaScript, PHP, jQuery, and Java. I also want to begin streaming and video producing on the side to have a fun hobby that can make me more "computer savvy."
